‚ÑπÔ∏è About Scoring

The suspicion score is calculated by summing weighted signals:

  • Criminal Record: Fixed points for prior convictions
  • Crime Window Calls: Points per call during crime time
  • High Transactions: Points for transactions above threshold
  • Bank CCTV: Points for sightings at crime location
  • Call Volume: Points for above-average activity
